## Change 

After the retrospective, our team decided to measure how well AI use has sped up our development, while also taking into consideration the possible drawbacks related to AI usage. Considering that the deadline is in a few days, it would be a mistake to not take advantage of every tool that is at our disposal. 

## Results
AI use has greatly sped up our development over the last sprint cycle. It also helps in explaining the code contributions of team members so that we are all up to speed on the codebase without spending many hours trying to understand how every line of code works. Many members find that AI can turn us into 10x engineers while others have some concerns that not fully understanding the code base could lead to a lack of understanding of the codebase which could lead to some roadblocks when debugging that the AI cannot figure out. Overall, we feel that using AI has been a success and has helped greatly in working towards our code freeze.

# Retro Outcomes 

### Our Retro Process
Our retro processes have followed a Start, Stop, Continue format. Every member suggests something that they want the team to start, stop, or continue doing. Then we vote on the items that our team members suggested and the top 3 are chosen and discussed. We then make a commitment to follow these items during the following sprint and report on the outcomes during the next retro.
